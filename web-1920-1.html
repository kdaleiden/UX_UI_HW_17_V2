ABOUT ME
Thank you for dropping by. My name is Kaitlyn and I am at my core, a passionate creative that I apply to my work in design, communications, and leading diverse teams.

My love for both technology and design started at a young age where I spent hours on our family Mac playing Oregon Trail and “designing” in Kid Pix. I studied Fine Art in college where I discovered my love for Graphic Design and spent the last 10 years working for creative teams in print, marketing and communications.

In 2020 I choose to enter the UT Austin UX/UI full-stack-development bootcamp where I acquired skills in lean UX research, design thinking, prototyping and interaction design.  

I am inspired by traveling to new places, getting lost in a museum gallery, or listening to a thought-provoking podcast. I am learning obsessed and am constantly striving to learn more about new design trends, the latest technology in my field and being a thoughtful and impactful leader.

I believe design should always have a seat at the table and to never underestimate the power of compelling design and good storytelling.
